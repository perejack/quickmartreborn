About MegaMart Plus

Our Story

Founded in 2010, MegaMart Plus has grown from a single store in Nairobi to become Kenya's most trusted retail chain. Our commitment to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for families across the nation.

With over 50 strategically located branches from Mombasa to Kisumu, Eldoret to Nakuru, we bring world-class shopping experiences to every corner of Kenya. Our extensive product range includes fresh produce, groceries, electronics, fashion, and home essentials.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional retail experiences that enhance the quality of life for all Kenyans while creating sustainable value for our employees, customers, and communities.
