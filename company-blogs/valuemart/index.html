Our Rapid Growth Story

ValueMart Express started as a small convenience store in Nairobi in 2018. Today, we're Kenya's fastest-growing retail chain with 40+ locations and ambitious expansion plans.

Our success is built on innovation, customer satisfaction, and smart business practices. We've revolutionized retail in Kenya by combining traditional values with cutting-edge technology.

From our humble beginnings to becoming a household name, ValueMart Express continues to set new standards in affordable quality and convenient shopping experiences.
